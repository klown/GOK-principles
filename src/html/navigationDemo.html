Palette Navigation Demonstration

This is a demonstration of using a "branch back stack" to keep track of one's location is in a navigation sequences of palettes and provide a means of returning to a previous palette.

Notes: Most keys on the palettes are non-functional in this demo. The exceptions are:

  • On Main palette, "Mouse" and "Menus" navigate to the Mouse and Menus palettes, respectively.
  • The Mouse palette does not provide any further forward navigation, and most of its keys are non-functional. The "Back" key goes back to the Main palette.
  • On the Menus palette, the "Edit" key is the only key that navigates forward, and the "Back" key goes back to the Main palette.
  • On the Edit palette, there is no further forward navigation, and the "Back" key goes back to the Menus palette.
